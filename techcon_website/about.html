Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for local developers and has since grown into a global tech conference attracting innovators from all over the world.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to empower technology professionals to collaborate, innovate, and drive meaningful change in the world. We focus on inclusivity, innovation, and impact.
